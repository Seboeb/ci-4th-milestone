About

Welcome to The Tasting Experience development portal! If you are having trouble with the Finder App or the Recipe Community website, you are in the right place. Here you can report bugs that you have found and share it with the developers of the Tasting Experience group. The cool thing is, is that you can request new features as well! Just submit your idea and get it supported by other users.

We ask a donation for new feature request, starting at € 150,00, since our team is spending at least half of its time to these. However, bugs are fixed for free!

Websites

The Tasting Experience - Finder App

Sometimes you just want to grab a nice drink - maybe with some delicious food - and still want a great experience for, lets say, just the midday or evening. Where do you look for such a place? Look no further and start using this App!

The Tasting Experience - Recipe Community

This community platform gives opportunity for users to share their tasting experiences. You can find recipes for nice drinks or cocktails, but you could also create your own recipes and share them with the rest of the userbase.

How does it work?

You can start right away, even without having an account. You can see the reported bugs and feature requests on the development page over here. However, if you want to contribute you will need to sign in. This has several advantages, you can place comments and participate with other users and place bug or feature request on your watchlist.

Bug Reports

When using the tasting experience services, it is possible that you encounter some functionallity that is not behaving as you would expect. You can report these so called bugs over here. It is also possible to upvote bug reports from other users. More upvotes results in a higher priority to solve by our development team. We always fix bugs for free.

Feature Request

If you have great ideas about new functionallity for the tasting experience services or you think some existing functionallity can be optimized, you can submit a feature request. Since feature requests require intensive development time (more than 50% overall), we ask for a donation with a minimum amount of € 150,00. Try to be as descriptive as possible and convience other users to support your feature request! When the donation target is reached, the development begins.

Active Development

You can actively contribute to bug reports and feature request by joining the conversations. Comment or reply on other users and provide them your thoughts about the subject.

Dashboard

On can create new bug reports and feature request in your personal dashboard. You can find it on the main development page. It provides a summary of your submitted reports and requests and a general overview of all (non)active development. If you are interested in some specific request, you can add them to your watchlist.
